## Relationships

He is devoted to The Sixth Storyteller, but oblivious to his casual nature, leading to a lot of awkward exchanges. He views [[Mae Seraphine Walker]] as a potentially dangerous rival, believing that she is undermining the status of the Storyteller.

He frequently meets with [[Florian]], both to relay information between him and the Storyteller, and to work directly under Florian on his projects.

### Affiliations

Leon is the firstborn of his generation in the Van Wolfe family, which is one of the oldest families registered in the [[Osterfeld]] Patriciaat, granting him status equivalent to the nobility within the state, and even a measure of special privilege throughout the rest of [[Osun Tanum]].
